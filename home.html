Interface Sketch & Key Components

Coffee Maker

  • Top lid

    Access to the filter cup and the water container

  • Filter cup

    Filtering coffee grounds

  • Water container

    Expecting water

  • Glass Coffee Holder

      • Allowing large volume of water coming in/out

  • Warming plate

    Keeping coffee warm

  • Power chord

Contextual Inquiry

Make sure you note general patterns in their interactions with the interface. How did the users react while using the interface? How does it differ from other users?

Prepare some questions for users regarding their experiences with the interface. Have questions prepared to get insight about their expectations, experience, motives, and challenges using the interface (steer the user to talk about the interface, not the product.

Prepared Questions:

  • What do you expect a coffee maker do?
  • How often do you use a coffee maker?
  • Why do you use a coffee maker, if you do?
  • Do you think it is easy to use?
  • What do you like about it?
  • What is something you find inconvenient?
  • Would you consider investing in a fancier coffee machine?
  • If this maker is broken will you get the same one again?
  • You ideal version of a coffee machine?
